About Job

Special program for the Employment of Students (SPES) The Special Program for the Employment of Students (SPES) of the DOLE-BLE is an employment bridging program during summer or Chrismas Vacation that aims to augment the family’s income of poor but deserving students, out-of-school youth (OSY) and dependents of displacedor would-be dispalced workers to continue and finish their education. While gaining experience and enhancing their capabilities as future members of the workforce, the program effectively hones the beneficiaries’ skills while molding their attitude and habits towards work in a positive manner.

Qualification Standards

  • At least 15 years old, but not more than 30 years old at the time of application
  • Preferably residing within Pila, Laguna
  • Currently enrolled in an educational institution, either public or private
  • Has no failing grade and has earned at least a passing general weighted average in the most recent academic year or term
  • Should be of good moral character
  • Proficient in performing basic computer operations and tasks
  • Must be fit for work

Requirements

  • SPES form with picture
  • ID pictures
  • Birth Certificate issued by the Philippine Statistics Authority
  • True Copy of Grades or Out-of-School Youth Certificate
  • Certificate of Indigency, Parent’s/Guardian’s ITR, and Certificate of Guardianship, if applicable
